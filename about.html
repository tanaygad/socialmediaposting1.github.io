Powerful APIs to View and Schedule Posts

The Social Media Content Automation project simplifies the creation, approval, and scheduling of social media content. It enables Club Artizen users to edit, or approve content before it's posted to Instagram or Facebook. The project emphasizes user-friendliness, efficiency, and aesthetic appeal, aiming to enhance social media management with backend support for authentication and secure posting.

View Post Templates ✔

Create a post template and verify the captions which can be further edited according to user's needs.

Post And Schedule ✔

Schedule your posts on mulitple platforms like Instagram and Facebook using our website. You can schedule your posts for a week, a month or even an entire year if you want.
