Tutorials

Step-by-step guides to help you make the most of our services. This tutorial outlines the process of using our posting template features:

  • From the home page, select a posting template from one of three categories: Blogs, Craft Stories, or Products.
  • After selecting a template, choose the desired date and time for your post.
  • You have the option to edit the caption of your post before scheduling it.
  • To view scheduled posts, navigate to the 'View Schedule' page accessible from our dynamic navbar located at the top left of the website.
  • On the 'View Schedule' page, you have the option to either edit the schedule or reject the post if you change your mind.
